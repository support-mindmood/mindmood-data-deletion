Data Deletion – MindMood

Last updated: October 16, 2025

Delete local app data

On your device, open the app and go to Settings → Factory reset. This erases all MindMood data stored locally (journal entries, moods, goals/affirmations, insights history, preferences).

AI Insights requests

When you tap Generate AI Insights, selected fields (for example mood, journal text, sleep hours, alcohol units, cigarettes) are sent securely to our processing provider only to return an insight. MindMood does not retain this data on its own servers.

If you believe a specific request was retained by a third-party processor and want it deleted, email us with the approximate date/time of the request so we can coordinate deletion consistent with our Privacy Policy.

Purchases (Pro access)

For purchases through Google Play, we may receive a purchase token to verify your Pro entitlement. We do not receive your card or billing details. If you need help removing a purchase entitlement, contact us.
